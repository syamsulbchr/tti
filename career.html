Career

With dedication, hard work, and the right qualifications, a career as an ISO consultant can be a great choice for those who enjoy helping organizations improve their processes and achieve their goals.

Career

Requirement

Job Responsibility (Included but not limited to):.

provide assistance regarding the international standard to be maintain in company

develop such policies that comply with the international standards

Build /Support to build the document system including working procedures, instructions and regulation to comply with ISO

Implement and manage an effective compliance program.

Advise management on the company’s compliance with laws and regulations through detailed reports.

Create and manage effective action plans in response to audit discoveries and compliance violations.

Regularly audit company procedures, practices, and documents to identify possible weaknesses or risks.

Assess company operations to determine compliance risk.

Ensure all employees are educated on the latest regulations and processes.

Job Types: Full-time, Contract Contract length: 12 months.

Job Requirements:

Bachelor’s degree in law, finance, business management, or a related field, fresh graduate welcome.

Proven experience as ISO Consultant or have involved in ISO Certification.

Good knowledge of legal requirements and procedures.

Brilliant oral and written communication skills in English.

Highly-analytical with strong attention to detail.

Location:

Bandung or Jakarta and able to travel around Indonesia.
